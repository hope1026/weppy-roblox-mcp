## Public Support

For questions, install issues, Pro activation problems, and confirmed bugs, open a public [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/hope1026/weppy-roblox-mcp/issues/new).

Please include:
- Your OS and Node.js version
- AI client you're using
- What you've already tried

Do not post raw license keys, receipts, email addresses, or personal billing details in public issues.
